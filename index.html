About

What is Huskara?

Our Huskara drink is a refreshing coffee based fruit drink that is made up of dried cherry coffee husks or "Cascara" which means “husk" or “peel” in spanish.



How is our drink made?

The pulped fruit is collected after the coffee beans have been removed from the cherries as part of coffee processing. The husks are then naturally sun-dried on raised beds, then milled and infused in water.


Our aim

To offer a refreshing and healthy drink for those who want something a little different.
