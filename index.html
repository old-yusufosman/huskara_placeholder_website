About

What is Huskara?

Huskara is a refreshing and fruity drink made from the dried skin of the coffee berry. This skin is called 'Cascara' (it's Spanish for 'husk').




How our drink is made

After the bean is removed from the coffee berry, the left-over husks are sun-dried on raised beds. The Cascara husks are steeped in cool spring water, before adding a hint of natural fruit juice for extra refreshment




Origin

All of our Cascara is hand-picked and sourced directly from the mountains of Peru, 2,100 metres above sea level.
