Introducing Cascara


Spanish for ‘peel’ or ‘husk’, Cascara is the bright red outer layer of the coffee berry.


Normally, when making coffee, the bean inside the coffee berry is harvested and roasted, and the nutrient rich outer peel is discarded. We were determined to harness the many health benefits of the berry and to increase the economic value of the coffee plant to local farmers - and that is why we developed a new soft drink called Huskara.

A Fresh Take


Huskara is a fresh take on the coffee plant - healthier and more refreshing than coffee, it harnesses the sweet antioxidant-rich outer layer of the coffee berry to produce a drink that is fruity and naturally caffeinated.

SUPERFOOD


With up to 4 times more antioxidants than blueberries, higher concentrations of Omega-3 essential fatty acids than chia seeds, and the same amount of protein found in eggs, Cascara is the latest superfood.

VITALISING


Huskara is naturally caffeinated (containing about a third of the caffeine found in coffee) and will give you the mental boost you need.

SUSTAINABLE


We source our single origin Organic cascara from local farmers in the mountains of Peru, 2,100 metres above sea level. Our vision is to increase the economic benefits of the coffee plant to local farmers, by buying something that is normally discarded.
